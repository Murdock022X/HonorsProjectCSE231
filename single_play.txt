Strategy Notes: 
The strategy I followed was the long chains rule, with this rule 
I created an algorithm that searches for chains. A chain being a 
sequence of moves that will take boxes one after another. In the 
single play games you can see this occur, player B will use moves 
that look like a winding snake. It starts with a box that has three 
sides full, from the open side there must be at least one more box 
to take, that box will have the side that will be taken by the first 
box open, as well as a new side that is open. Some chains are only 
two moves long and some will fill in half of a large board.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Welcome to Single Play Mode. Player A is the random player, 
Player B is the winning player for all games and game modes. This 
first game will have Player A go first, then a second game will occur 
with Player B going first.
